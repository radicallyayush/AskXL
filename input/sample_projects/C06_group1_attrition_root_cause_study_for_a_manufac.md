## Background
Voluntary attrition has crept up over three consecutive quarters, and leadership wants a data-backed explanation before the next review cycle.

## Objective
Develop a data-grounded recommendation the client can act on within one operating cycle, using the frameworks and tools covered in HR Analytics.

## Scope & Approach
1. Combine HRIS, survey, and exit-interview data
2. Test candidate drivers of attrition statistically
3. Rank drivers by impact and actionability
4. Propose targeted retention interventions

## Deliverables
- Final written report (10-12 pages, excluding annexures)
- 15-minute client-style presentation deck
- Supporting data workbook with all calculations
- One-page executive summary for leadership
