## Background
Inventory swings between stockouts and overstock have made the client's planning team question its current forecasting approach.

## Objective
Develop a data-grounded recommendation the client can act on within one operating cycle, using the frameworks and tools covered in Business Statistics.

## Scope & Approach
1. Assemble and clean historical demand data
2. Compare forecasting models (moving average, exponential smoothing, ARIMA)
3. Validate forecast accuracy against a held-out period
4. Recommend a model and a review cadence

## Deliverables
- Final written report (10-12 pages, excluding annexures)
- 15-minute client-style presentation deck
- Supporting data workbook with all calculations
- One-page executive summary for leadership
